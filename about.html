About Oculi Deum

Oculi Deum means the eye of God. Until the 15th Century, many astrologists believed that the Earth was the center of the Universe, with all planets and the Sun revolving around it. The belief was that as the center of the Universe, the Earth and humans were the eyes of God. While this has proven to be false almost a half-a-millenium ago and Oculi Deum takes a more humanist approach to our work, we believe that as inhabitors of this Planet, we are gods in our own making and are responsible for creating a better world, a healthier world to leave for our children.

Here at Oculi Deum, we work with neighboring communities to create neighborhood gardens and educate the people of how to better care for our planet as individuals. The work is not individual, but we can start with one person to create a world that we can be proud of. We build the gardens from the ground-u, then give away fresh produce to the neighborhood through food drives. However, everyone is welcome to buy plants and produce either in-person or on our Online Store

Oculi Deum is an independant produce store, meaning we do not take any money from big private corporations. We partner with the community and other non-profits that have a similar message. Therefore, to keep out operations going, we rely on people like you that can make our work possible. Please visit our gardens, as well as shop HERE!
